Project Deliverables:
  • Scripts for loading data from and updating data into MongoDB Database.
  • APIs for updating middleware databases and local databases.
  • Background Engine Server at the Middleware to accept queries and add the CRDTs.
  • API for communication between Background Engine Servers for deciding when to reach a consensus. (Planned as a substitute for the authoritative Central Server, shall be considered as an afterthought)
  • CRDT functionalities to bring consensus among Middleware database instances.
  • Interfaces for showing the status of databases and provide the option for syncing, etc.
